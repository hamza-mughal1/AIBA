## What It Does

It thinks, plans, discovers, distributes, searches, verifies, and synthesizes — autonomously.

It uses swarm intelligence to handle complex objectives by breaking them into smaller tasks, executing them in parallel, and weaving the results back together.

_The scope of what it can do is tied to your imagination_

---

## Some Use Cases

- Monitor changes across sites and surfaces you care about — on a schedule, without lifting a finger.
- Research across dozens of sources simultaneously, cross-verify every finding, and walk away with a single synthesized report.
- Run QA workflows against live web applications — forms, flows, visual regressions — automatically.
- Perform personal brand threat intelligence — scheduled scans for exposure, mentions, and impersonation across platforms.
- Track competitors over time — pricing shifts, positioning changes, feature launches, public signals.
- Extract structured intelligence from surfaces that resist automation.

---

## How It Works

1. Choose a mode — a single agent for depth, or a swarm for breadth.
2. Describe your goal.
3. AIBA plans an approach, executes it, adapts as it learns, and delivers the result.
4. The conversation stays open. Refine. Pivot. Dig deeper.
5. Save your session, or schedule the whole thing to run again without you.

---

## AIBA-beats (It Runs While You Don't)

Beats are scheduled autonomous runs. Configure a beat once. AIBA wakes up, does the work, logs everything, and sends you the report.
